ABOUT ME

I am an undergraduate student at the University of Waterloo with a major in Health Studies, a Health Informatics Specialization, and a Computer Science Minor. With the exponential growth of technology usage and advancement in our society, my goal is to implement technology into health care. My passion involves Web Development and developing mobile applications.

I was born and raised in Mississauga and eventually enrolled into the Health Studies program at the University of Waterloo. At first I did not know what to pursue and so I challenged myself to prepare for the MCAT and eventually apply for medical school. However, that all changed when I took a health informatics course and realized the massive potential of technology usage in the field of health care. I eagerly wanted to learn programming languages and mobile application development. I began to teach myself programming languages such as Java, Python, HTML/CSS, and JavaScript while learning programs such as SAS, SPSS, DrRacket, and more Python at the University of Waterloo. Since then, I have been working on web pages (such as this one) and other projects to advance my skills as a full stack developer.
